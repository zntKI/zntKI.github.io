About Me

I am a first-year Game Engineer student at Saxion CMGT.
My professional passions revolve around Graphics and Gameplay programming, with a keen enthusiasm for Mathematics and Physics.

When it comes to my personal qualities, I believe to be a creative, disciplined and determined person.

Click here to find my CV

My journey in programming and mathematics began early on in my life, eventually leading me to attend the Highschool of Mathematics in Varna. With my growing interest in the field of software engineering, I decided to enroll in a C# Web development degree in an online academy by the name of SoftUni.

Video games have long been a deer hobby of mine, and having graduated both from high school and the C# Web development degree, I made the decision to combine my two passions and to delve into game development by enrolling in CMGT at Saxion. In my first year, I learned how to apply my C# knowledge to game programming by utilizing a custom-made 2D game engine and then Unity. Beyond programming, I engaged in supplementary courses covering game and UI/UX design, as well as professional skills like communication and research.
