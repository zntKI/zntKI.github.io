Background

My journey in programming and mathematics began early on in my life, eventually leading me to attend the Highschool of Mathematics in Varna. With my growing interest in the field of software engineering, I decided to enroll in a C# Web development degree in an online academy by the name of SoftUni.

Video games have long been a deer hobby of mine, and having graduated both from high school and the C# Web development degree, I made the decision to combine my two passions and to delve into game development by enrolling in CMGT at Saxion.

First year

In my first year at Saxion, I learned how to apply my C# knowledge to gameplay, physics and algorithms programming by utilizing a custom-made 2D game engine.
Attending these courses, I have now established strong foundations, on which I can confidently build upon in the future.

Additionaly, at the end of the year, I had the chance to delve deeper into the Unity game engine, creating my first ever fully functional 3D game.
It was then, when for the first time, I had to manage a project of bigger scale. As a consequence, I had to apply various techniques to keep my project healthy and maintainable.
I learned valuable lessons regarding software architecture, but this experience also got me excited to explore more concepts that would facilitate the development process in the future.

Beyond programming, I engaged in supplementary courses covering Game and UI/UX design, as well as professional skills like communication and research.

Group projets

Throughout the year, I had the chance to participate in several group projects, collaborating with amazing people to create an unique game in two weeks.
Given the short time period, we faced some adversaties, however, by maintaining a good working environment and always communicating, we were able to overcome them and achieve our goals.
